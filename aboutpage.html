About Myself

Hello, I'm Jenny, currently pursuing a B.A. in European Culture and Society at Flensburg University. While my academic path may not align perfectly with my deepest interests, my journey took an unexpected turn when I returned from a transformative year in England in 2021.

My true passion lies in the dynamic realms of Game Design, Web Development, and Graphic Design. Beyond my studies, I immerse myself in the world of creativity and technology, fueled by a genuine enthusiasm for bringing ideas to life through digital mediums.

During moments of leisure, you'll often find me engrossed in captivating books, navigating virtual landscapes through gaming, or indulging in the spine-chilling allure of horror movies. Beyond the digital realm, I embrace the analog pleasures of culinary artistry, occasionally treating myself and others to my culinary creations. Additionally, I find joy in nurturing my collection of plants, fostering a green oasis that mirrors my appreciation for growth and creativity.
